Glif

Glyph style themes. Mix and Match 5 Icon Sets. Choose 1 or use all 5 together. Arrange the priority order of Icon sets 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 in Snowboard when used together. Highest priority goes to top of order arrangement. Best to use all 5 together to fill the gap of missing icons. Use Glif icons by themself or with any of the included Icon BG colors with shadow AE effect.

  • 5000+ Glyph Icons
  • 5 icon sets that can be mixed and matched with each other using Snowboard priority arrangement
  • Settings Icons
